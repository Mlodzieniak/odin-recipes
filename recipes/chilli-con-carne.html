Chilli con Carne

Description

Very tasty dish that contains mainly corn, tomatoes and beans!

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Corn
  • Beaf
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Fry onion on frying pan and using medium heat
  2. Add beaf and mince all everything
  3. Add salt, pepper, oregano
  4. Mix everything with tomatoes, beans and corn
  5. Cook under cover for 15 minutes
